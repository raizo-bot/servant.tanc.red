FAQ

What is Servant?

Servant is a bot for Discord, a VoIP application. She will help you to improve your Discord server by organizing and moderating channels, roles and members and by entertaining your members.

The name of this bot refers to the anime series Fate where masters fight a war with the help of heroic spirits called servants. The character I chose is better known as Saber or Artoria Pendragon, but I decided to take the more general title "Servant", as a Discord bot is also kind of a servant.

Why should I use Servant?

Servant is designed to satisfy all your needs for you Discord server. I personally don't like to have a ton of bots on my server, so I decided to create one, that can do almost everything. While doing this, it was very important to me, that each feature is high quality as many multipurpose Discord bots have many but poorly designed features.

How to get started?

First of all, you may invite Servant to your Discord server. After that you should take a look into !help or Commands to see everything Servant can do. If you need a detailed help for a command, you can either type the command without any arguments, e.g. !avatar or you can look up Commands once again. To set the basic settings on your server (Language, Prefix, Offset/Timezone), you may run !serversetup.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Join Servant's Kingdom (support server) and head to the corresponding section.
